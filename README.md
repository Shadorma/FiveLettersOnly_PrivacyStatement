Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 1, 2025
Game: Five Letters Only
Developer: Shadorma Game Studios

This Privacy Policy describes how information is accessed and used when you play Five Letters Only (“the Game”) through Valve’s Steam platform.

1. Information Accessed via Steam

The Game does not independently collect, store, or process personal data.
When played through Steam, the Game accesses limited information provided by Steamworks APIs, which may include:

Steam display name
Steam avatar image
Steam ID (used internally by Steam for account identification)
Leaderboard scores and rankings
Achievement status
Steam Cloud save data (game progress and settings)
This information is made available to the Game solely by Steam and remains under Valve’s control.

2. Purpose of Access

The information accessed through Steamworks is used only to enable core gameplay and platform functionality, including:

Displaying player names and avatars on in-game leaderboards
Uploading and retrieving leaderboard scores
Unlocking and displaying achievements
Saving and loading game progress via Steam Cloud

The Game does not use this information for analytics, advertising, marketing, profiling, or tracking.

3. Data Storage and Processing

All personal and account-related data is stored and processed by Valve Corporation as part of the Steam platform.
The developer does not maintain external databases or servers containing user data.
The developer does not retain copies of Steam account information outside of Steam.
For details on how Valve processes data, please review Valve’s Privacy Policy:
https://store.steampowered.com/privacy_agreement/

4. Data Sharing

The Game does not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.
No data is transmitted to external services outside of Steamworks.

5. Children’s Privacy

The Game does not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. Access to the Game and its online features is governed by Steam’s account and age requirements.

6. User Rights and Data Requests

Because all personal and account data is managed by Steam, requests regarding access, correction, or deletion of such data must be directed to Valve through the Steam platform.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the Game’s data usage changes. The effective date above will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the Game after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
